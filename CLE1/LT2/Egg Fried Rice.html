Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice (as much as you you fancy really!)
  • Half an onion (Or more, again if you fancy it)
  • A tin of sweetcorn
  • A few mushrooms
  • 1-2 eggs

Methods

1) Start cooking the rice however ever you do, and put some oil in a frying pan.

2) Chop the onion into bits, any size really, doesn't matter particularly and thrown into the frying pan.

3) Chop the mushrooms and add the onion.

4) Once the rice is cooked, drain it off and add it into the frying pan.

5) Tip the dained sweetcorn into the panas well stir it all up.

6) Break the eggs into the pan (Or beat them together in another bowl and tip into the frying pan) and stir the mixture up.

7) At this point, you need to keep stirring the mixture, keeping the eggs moving. Keep going until the eggs have cooked, should take about 5-8 minutes maybe?
